My Process

A streamlined workflow to deliver exceptional results

1. Discovery & Brief

Understanding your vision, goals, and requirements through detailed consultation.

2. Asset Review

Reviewing all footage, audio, and materials to plan the editing approach.

3. Rough Cut

Creating the initial edit with basic cuts, structure, and pacing.

4. Refinement

Adding transitions, effects, color grading, and audio enhancement.

5. Client Review

Presenting the draft for feedback and incorporating requested changes.

6. Final Delivery

Delivering the final video in all required formats and resolutions.
